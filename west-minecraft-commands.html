West Minecraft — Command Cheat Sheet

Click a section to expand. Copy buttons work in modern browsers.

HOME
Homes Save spots and teleport back
Tip: Name homes something obvious like mine, village, base.
/sethome [home name]
Create a home at your current location.
/home [home name]
Teleport to one of your homes.
WARP
Warps Server-made destinations
Note: Warps are set by admins. Ask a mod if you want one added.
/warp [warp name]
Teleport to a server warp.
CLAIM
Land Claims (GriefPrevention) Protect your base and share access
Basics: Claim with a golden shovel. Visualize borders with a stick (investigation tool).
/trust [player]
Full build permission inside your claim.
/accesstrust [player]
Access to doors, buttons, levers, beds.
/containertrust [player]
Access to chests/containers and other “inventory” interactions.
/permissiontrust [player]
Allows them to share their trust level with others.
/trustlist
See who has access to your current claim.
/untrust [player]
Remove a player’s permissions from the claim you’re in.
/untrust all
Remove everyone’s permissions from the claim you’re in.
Big hammer
/abandonclaim
Delete the claim you’re currently standing in.
Deletes claim
/abandonallclaims
Delete all of your claims.
Deletes everything
/claimslist
List your claims and claim-block info.
/claiminfo
Shows information about the claim you’re standing in.
/subdivideclaims
Switch shovel into sub-claim mode (plots inside a bigger claim).
/basicclaims
Return shovel to normal claim mode.
/restrictsubclaim
Make new sub-claims NOT inherit parent permissions (useful for private rooms).
/claimexplosions
Toggle whether explosions affect your claim.
TPA
Teleport Requests Ask first — don’t spam
Etiquette: Always ask in chat before sending requests.
/tpa [player]
Request to teleport to another player.
/tpahere [player]
Request that a player teleport to you.
/tpaccept
Accept a teleport request.
/tpdeny
Deny a teleport request.
/tpacancel
Cancel a request you sent.
/tpatoggle
Toggle incoming requests on/off.
GRAVE
Death Chests (GravesX) Find your grave and recover items
Teleporting: GravesX supports an OP teleport command, but players on this server shouldn’t have it. Use the coordinates + compass guidance instead.
/graves
Open your graves menu / info (coordinates, tracking, etc.).
/graves help
Shows plugin info and help.
If a command is not working, ask a server administrator.
